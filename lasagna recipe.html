Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  3. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet
  4. Add tomato sauce and simmer
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish
  7. Repeat layers
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes
  10. Let lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving

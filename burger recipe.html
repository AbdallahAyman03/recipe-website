Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper
  2. Form into patties and cook on a grill or stovetop
  3. Add cheddar cheese to patties and cook until melted
  4. Toast burger buns
  5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, mayonnaise, and ketchup
